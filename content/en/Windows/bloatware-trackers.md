## Windows10Debloater

Script to remove Windows 10 bloatware. [Download](https://github.com/Sycnex/Windows10Debloater)

```powershell
iwr -useb https://git.io/debloat|iex
```

## O&O ShutUp10

Free antispy tool for Windows 10 and 11. [Download](https://www.oo-software.com/shutup10)

```powershell
iwr -Uri "https://dl5.oo-software.com/files/ooshutup10/OOSU10.exe" -OutFile "OOSU10.exe"
```

## Privatezilla

Privatezilla is the simplest way to perform a quick privacy and security check of your Windows 10 copy. [Download](https://github.com/builtbybel/privatezilla)
